Privacy Policy

Mesh Sports LLC  ·  Last updated: July 2026  ·  Version: Beta 1.0

The short version: We collect what we need to run the platform. We don't sell your data. We don't advertise to you. Student data is treated with extra care. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

1. Who This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Mesh Sports, operated by Mesh Sports LLC ("we," "us," "our"). This includes head coaches, assistant coaches, players, and parents or guardians who use the platform.

If you have questions, contact us at support@meshsports.co.

2. What Data We Collect

Category What we collect Why
Account data Email address, password (hashed), role (coach / player / parent) To create and authenticate your account
Program data School name, team name, sport, team colors, logo To configure your program in the app
Roster data Player names, jersey numbers, positions, grades, contact information To manage your program roster
Activity data Attendance records, game stats, performance logs, announcements, schedule events To provide the core features of the platform
Usage data Which features you use, approximate session frequency To understand how the product is used and improve it
TOS acceptance Timestamp and version of Terms of Service accepted at signup To maintain a record of consent

We do not collect payment information during the beta period. We do not collect precise location data. We do not use tracking pixels or third-party advertising cookies.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain the Mesh Sports platform.
  • Authenticate users and protect account security.
  • Deliver features such as announcements, schedules, and roster management.
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset) via our email provider, Resend.
  • Identify and fix bugs during the beta period.
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the product (using anonymized data only).

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties under any circumstances.

4. Who We Share Data With

We share your data with the following service providers, solely to operate the platform:

Provider Purpose Data shared
Supabase Database and authentication (US-based) All program and user data
Resend Transactional email delivery Email address only
Cloudflare Web hosting and content delivery IP address (standard web traffic logs)

We do not share your data with any other third parties. We will never share student data with advertisers, data brokers, or any party not listed above.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Mesh Sports is a platform for high school sports programs, which means many users are minors under the age of 18.

Accounts for children under 13: COPPA applies to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from a child under 13, and we do not create a login account for a child under 13 without verifiable consent from a parent or guardian. If a coach adds a player under 13 to their roster, that player's information is entered by the coach and is visible only within that program.

Player accounts for ages 13 and older: Players who are 13 or older may create their own player account when invited by a coach. COPPA does not require parental consent for this age group, and we do not require it to activate their account. In all cases, a coach controls who belongs to their program, and a parent or guardian may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's data as described below.

What we collect from minors: Name, jersey number, position, grade, and any stats or attendance data entered by coaches. We do not collect more information from minors than is necessary to operate the program.

Parent rights: Parents and guardians may at any time request to review, correct, or delete their child's data by emailing support@meshsports.co. We will respond within 5 business days.

No advertising to minors: We never use data from minors for advertising purposes, and we never share it with advertising platforms.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Our security practices include:

  • All data is stored in Supabase, which encrypts data at rest and in transit.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text.
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies, meaning users can only access data belonging to their own program.
  • Our service role key (used for administrative database operations) is stored only in server-side Edge Functions, never in client-side code.
  • HTTPS is enforced for all connections.

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of becoming aware of the incident.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your data is retained for 30 days to allow for recovery if the deletion was accidental.
  • After 30 days, all program data, roster data, and personal information associated with your account is permanently deleted from our systems.
  • Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely as they cannot be linked back to you.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — you can export your program data at any time from within the app.
  • Correct your data — you can update your account information at any time.
  • Delete your data — you can delete your account from the app, or email us to request deletion.
  • Withdraw consent — you can stop using the platform at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@meshsports.co.

9. Cookies

Mesh Sports uses minimal cookies. We use a session cookie to keep you logged in between visits. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
