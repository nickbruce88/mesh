# Mesh — Demo Program Setup (for marketing screenshots)

A ready-to-build fictional program. Goal: **believable, full, clean, one consistent identity + theme.**
All data is fictional — no real people, no real school. Import what you can; type the rest.

> **Theme:** pick **one** (light or dark) and shoot everything in it.
> **Capture device:** coach planning views on **desktop**, player/parent views on **phone**.

## 4. Practice plan — build one rich Tuesday (your HERO shot)
Practice → today → **start time 3:30 PM**. Name your columns QB / RB / WR / TE / OL / DL / LB / DB
and assign a coach to each (from staff above). Build these periods top to bottom:

| # | Time | Min | Type | Detail |
|---|------|-----|------|--------|
| P1 | 3:30 | 15 | Team | **Dynamic Warmup + Stretch** — all groups, south end |
| T | 3:45 | 2 | Transition | to Indy |
| P2 | 3:47 | 15 | Group (Indy) | QB: 3-step timing · RB: ball-security circuit · WR: release vs press · TE: blocking angles · OL: pass set / hands · DL: get-off + bags · LB: drops + fill · DB: backpedal + break |
| W | 4:02 | 3 | Water | — |
| P3 | 4:05 | 15 | Group | **Inside Run** (OL/DL/RB/LB) ‖ **Skelly** (QB/WR/TE/DB) |
| T | 4:20 | 2 | Transition | to Team |
| P4 | 4:22 | 20 | Team | **Offense vs Scout D** — install: Dart / Bolt _(add a cell note: "emphasize backside cut-off")_ |
| W | 4:42 | 3 | Water | — |
| P5 | 4:45 | 15 | Team | **Defense vs Scout O** — Cover 3 install |
| P6 | 5:00 | 10 | Team | **Special Teams** — Punt / Punt Return |
| P7 | 5:10 | 10 | Team | **2-Minute Drill** — situational |

Extra polish: attach 1–2 drills from your Drill Library to Indy cells, drop **one cell note**, and give
one period a **row color**. That makes the grid look genuinely used.

**Drill Library** — add ~6 so the picker isn't empty: "Inside Zone Fit", "Mesh Point Reads",
"Release vs Press", "Pass Pro Ladder", "Tackling Circuit", "Cover 3 Bail".

## 6. Messaging — needs a few real accounts
Roster players don't need logins for the roster/depth/practice shots. **Messaging does.** Minimum:
create **1 assistant coach**, **1 player**, and **1 parent** account (via your join codes) so you can shoot:

1. **Staff group thread** — "Varsity Staff": 
   - Coach Reeves: "Tuesday plan is up — heavy on Cover 3 install."
   - Coach Foster: "Good. I'll script scout looks for the DB period."
   - Coach Malone: "Adding a red-zone period Thursday."
2. **Broadcast** (All Players — auto-exists, post solo):
   - "Buses leave at 4:15 sharp Friday for Ridgeline. Eat before you get here. 🏈" _(this emoji is fine in a message)_
3. **Witnessed player DM** — the safety differentiator (parent is the witness):
   - Coach Reeves → player Elijah Grant, witness = his parent:
   - Coach: "Great film session today — your leverage on the boundary was much better."
   - Player: "Thanks coach, I'll keep working the backpedal drill."
   - ← screenshot this one with the **witness safety banner visible**.

## Capture checklist (in your chosen theme)
**Desktop (coach):** hero practice grid · depth chart · roster · a player card (Overview tab) · inventory
(with QR) · calendar (month view) · performance leaderboard.
**Phone:** player home · schedule · the witnessed DM (banner visible) · a broadcast · parent "My Player".

## Avoid
Real names/photos/contacts · a real school's name or logo · any "test/asdf" leftovers · empty states ·
harsh discipline/academic notes on player cards · switching themes mid-shoot · 50-row clutter (28 is plenty).
